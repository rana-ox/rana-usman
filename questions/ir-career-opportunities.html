Career Opportunities After IR

A degree in International Relations opens doors to prestigious careers both in the public sector and international organizations.

A
Diplomacy & Foreign Service The most sought-after path is joining the Foreign Service of Pakistan (FSP) or working as a diplomat to represent the country's interests abroad.
B
International NGOs & UN Graduates can work for the United Nations, World Bank, or organizations like Amnesty International in roles related to policy analysis and humanitarian aid.
C
Intelligence & Analysis Large corporations and government agencies hire IR graduates as Intelligence Analysts or Risk Consultants to navigate global security and economic trends.
"In an increasingly globalized world, IR graduates are the bridge between local challenges and international solutions."
