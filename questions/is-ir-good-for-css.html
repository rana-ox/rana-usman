Is International Relations Good for the CSS Exam?

International Relations (IR) is widely considered one of the most strategic optional subjects for the CSS exam in Pakistan. It offers a unique overlap with several compulsory papers.

1
Overlapping Syllabus IR shares significant content with "Current Affairs" and "Pakistan Affairs." Preparing for IR effectively covers nearly 40% of these compulsory subjects.
2
High Scoring Potential In the 2026 examination trend, IR continues to be a "scoring" subject (200 marks) because it allows for analytical writing, which examiners prefer over rote memorization.
3
Essay Writing Advantage Most CSS Essay topics revolve around global politics, security, and economics. An IR background provides the theories and vocabulary needed to write a professional, high-level essay.
